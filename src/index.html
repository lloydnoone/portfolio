SunChaser

The idea for this app came from a persistent problem of living in the UK, a lack of sunny weather and mostly cloudy skies. My solution was to provide people with a one touch button that would find the closest city in the UK with sunny weather. It would then calculate the route to that city on public transport and display that information to the user.

Cigar Reviews

I made my own simple API, a database using MongoDB, bcrypt for authentication and React for the front-end. It was a solo project and I had two days to quickly put it together. It is for cigar reviews. Users are able to view cigars, register, login, add, edit comment on and remove cigars in the database served up by the API.
